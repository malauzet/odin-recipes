Chicken Curry

Description

This is a quick and flavorful chicken curry dish, perfect for a weeknight dinner. Tender pieces of chicken breast are cooked with onions and a blend of aromatic spices,curry, cumin and chili pepper, then finished with a light cream sauce for a smooth and comforting result.

Ingredients

For 4 people:

  • 2 yellow onions
  • curry
  • cumin
  • chili pepper
  • pepper
  • salt
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1 pot of light sour cream

Steps

  1. Heat a large frying pan. Cut the onions into small pieces and cook over fairly high heat.
  2. Stir, adding curry and cumin.
  3. Cut the chicken breasts into pieces, add them to the pan and add more spices; stir.
  4. Lower the heat and add 2 tablespoons of cream.
  5. After 5 minutes of cooking, add 2 more tablespoons of cream and spices (if necessary).
  6. If the dish is prepared in advance, add a little cream when reheating as the sauce evaporates.
  7. Enjoy!
